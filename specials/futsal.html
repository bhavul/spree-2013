Futsal

(Special event)

One of Spree’s biggest attractions last year, Futsal is 5-a-side football. Characterized by its blinding pace, the sheer creativity on display and the volume of goals, if you’re a football fan, this is one event not to be missed.


  • Each team will be given a team id with which they will be referred to during the tournament.
  • Each match in the group games qf and pqf will be for 20 minutes - without half time.
  • Each match in the semi finals and finals only will be for 30 minutes (with half time). In case of tie, extra time of 10 minutes will be played. If the match doesn't get decided even during extra time, a penalty shoot-out(4 a side) will decide the winner and then followed by sudden death.
  • Pink (2 minutes out) and red cards will be used during the tournament.
  • Any team will be allowed to play a match with 7 players at the maximum and 5 players minimum, the substitutions are rolling.
  • No player will be allowed to play in more than one team.
  • Players will not be allowed to play bare foot or with studs, they must have shoes (not studs).
  • Each team will be awarded with 3 points for a win and 1 point for a draw.
  • To decide the group standings, the team with max points stand first, if two teams have same points then goal difference decides who goes first, if there is a tie in goal difference also then, the team scoring more goals will go through. In case of tie at this point also, a penalty shoot out folowed by sudden death (as above) will decide the standings.
  • Any misconduct during a match, either on court or off court will lead to immediate disqualification of the team involved. All the decisions regarding this disqualifications will be taken by organising committee.
  • In case a match gets stopped before completion due to any reason, it will be resumed from the point of stoppage later with the same score and possession.


Pink Cards
A player who picks up a pink card will have to stay out for 2 minutes.
Hand push, harsh tackles, deliberate high clearences, handling ball inside D, abusing opponent player, disputes and fight with crowd.

Red Cards A player who picks up a red card will have to stay out for 10 minutes.
Continuous argument with refree or abusing referee.
Handling shots on target deliberately.
Assaulting opponent player.

Time Out
Can be opted only when the team has possession.
League matches - 1 time out per team.
Knock Out matches - 1 time out per team per half.

Extra Time
To be played only in knockout matches(semifinal and Final) if the scores are tied even after normal time.
Will extend to 5 minutes.

Penalty Shoot Out
To be played only during the knockout matches if the scores are tied(AFTER NORMAL TIME IN CASE OF QF AND PQF) even after extra time.
Both the teams will be given 4 penalities each.

Sudden Death
To be played only during the knockout matches if the scores are tied even after the penality shootout.

Hand Balls
Will not be considered a foul if the hand is a part of the body.
Will be considered a foul only if the hand is raised, and penality will be given for handballs inside the D.
If the handball is intended to stop a goal, red card will be awarded.

Miscellaneous
There will be no off sides.
A player can shoot @ the goal only from inside the half.
if a shot taken from outside the half deflects of an opponent player and goes inside the post, it wont be considered a goal.
In the above case, if the ball deflects of 2 opponent players and goes inside the post, it will be considered an own goal.
All free-kicks, goal kicks, and corners should be taken with the ball dead.

Spree

The Annual Sports Festival of the BITS Pilani, K.K. Birla Goa Campus.

In a quest to unravel the spirit of sporting competition and promote the fortitude of true sportsmanship, SPREE offers a plethora of fiercely contested events. To add to the sporting exhilaration, a special event is organized alongside each major sports event. SPREE 2011 saw Cricket accompanied by Gully Cricket, Football with Futsal, Basketball with 3BA, Volleyball with 3vs3 and Kick Volleyball, Lawn Tennis with Mini court Tennis, Table Tennis with Doubles, Carrom with C-Pool, Badminton with Mixed Doubles and Chess with anti-Chess. A diverse mix of off-beat events such as Pool, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, and Go-Karting added to the fervor. SPREE 2012 shall also unveil the Goa Marathon this year round.

SPREE 2011 witnessed as many as 1000 participants from colleges all over the country fighting it out for glory including colleges from as far as Kashmir. And this time, SPREE 2012 is all set to take the country by storm. And as if the heart pounding thrills and spills of the on-field action just aren't enough, SPREE presents an array of Special Nights featuring mesmerizing performances from renowned artists from all across the globe. Karaoke and DJ nites add to the enthrallment of the fest. In addition to this, crowds were spellbound by adrenalin pumping performances by African acrobats, jugglers and fire performers as well.

SPREE 2012 shall witness the best of sporting talent battle it out to clinch the crown of glory. The campus, equipped with state of the art sporting facilities of Badminton courts, Cricket ground, Football field, Futsal arena and Volleyball courts is all set to set the dais of sporting extravaganza ablaze, this March 2012. With an exciting line-up of sporting clashes coupled with additional special events, 'SPREE 2012 – Battle for Survival' promises to take the notch of sheer sporting talent a notch higher.
